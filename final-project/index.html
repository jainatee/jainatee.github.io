SPOTLIGHT

AMANDA PHINGBODHIPAKKIYA

A ballerina turned neuroscientist turned creative director, Phingbodhipakkiya has found a multitude of ways to pair design with science and entrepreneurism and more.

Case in point? Beyond Curie, the design project she's created to highlight badass women in science, technology, engineering and mathematics-women who haven't had as bright spotlight on them as Marie Curie, and who deserve recognition and praise for their work. If the images look familiar, it may be because Beyond Curie has has been featured in places like Fast Company, Smithsonian Magazine and Glamour and recently partnered with the March for Science.
